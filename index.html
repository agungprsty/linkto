Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about Linkto. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team.

What is Linkto?

Linkto is the ultimate link-in-bio platform designed for creators, affiliates, and businesses. Unlike traditional link-in-bio tools, Linkto combines beautiful Neo Brutalism design with powerful monetization features — including an affiliate product hub, deep analytics, and custom domains. It's everything you are, in one link.

Is Linkto really free?

Yes! Our Free plan includes unlimited links, basic analytics, and one theme — forever. No time limit, no credit card required. When you're ready to grow, you can upgrade to Starter ($5/mo), Pro ($12/mo), or Premium ($24/mo) for advanced features like custom domains, monetization tools, and priority support.

Can I use my own domain?

Absolutely! Custom domains are available on the Pro plan and above. Connect your own domain (like link.yourname.com) to your Linkto page — it takes less than 5 minutes to set up with our simple DNS configuration guide.

How does the Affiliate Hub work?

The Affiliate Hub lets you create a visual storefront directly on your Linkto page. Simply paste a product link from supported marketplaces (Shopee, Tokopedia, Amazon, etc.) and our smart auto-scraper will fetch the image, title, and price automatically. You can organize products into categories, set custom prices, and track conversions — all from one dashboard.

Can I migrate from another platform?

Yes! We offer a one-click import tool that lets you migrate your links from Linktree, Bio.link, and other major platforms. Your links, themes, and settings transfer seamlessly. Most users complete the migration in under 2 minutes.

What kind of analytics do you provide?

Our analytics dashboard tracks page views, link clicks, click-through rates, traffic sources, geographic data, and device information. Pro and Premium plans unlock advanced analytics including conversion tracking, revenue attribution, custom date ranges, and exportable reports. You can also integrate Meta, TikTok, and Google Pixels for advanced retargeting.
