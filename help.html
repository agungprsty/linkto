Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a Linkto account?

Creating an account is free and takes less than 60 seconds. Click "Sign Up Free" in the top right corner, enter your email and create a password, or sign up instantly with Google or Apple. Once registered, you can immediately start customizing your Linkto page.

How do I add affiliate products to my page?

With our Smart Affiliate Scraper, you can paste a product link from supported marketplaces (Shopee, Tokopedia, Amazon, etc.) directly into the "Add Product" button in your dashboard. We'll automatically fetch the image, title, and price. You can also manually add products and organize them into categories.

Can I use my own custom domain?

Yes! Custom domains are available on the Pro plan and above. Simply go to your Settings > Custom Domain, enter your domain, and update your DNS records. We provide a simple step-by-step guide. Once verified, your Linkto page will be accessible via your own domain.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard under Settings > Billing. Your paid features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the Free plan. There are no cancellation fees.

How do I track my link clicks and analytics?

Analytics are available on all plans. You can view your stats by clicking on "Analytics" in the dashboard sidebar. The Free plan includes basic analytics (views and clicks). Pro and Premium plans unlock advanced analytics including traffic sources, geographic data, device breakdown, and conversion tracking.

Is my data safe with Linkto?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We never sell your personal information. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
